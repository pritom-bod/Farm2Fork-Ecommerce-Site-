Our Journey

From a single farmer’s dream to a movement trusted across Bangladesh.

2018: The Idea

A group of passionate growers and technologists united to connect honest produce directly to customers, cutting out the middlemen.

2020: Growth Begins

First 50 partner farms join. Our deliveries spread to 10 districts and hundreds of doorsteps.

2022: Full Bangladesh Coverage

Advanced logistics, cold chain, and traceability introduce “zero compromise” freshness nationwide.

2025: Community Impact

Farm2Fork now empowers 1000+ farmers, feeds thousands daily, and leads in digital food innovation for Bangladesh!
